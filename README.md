## 🕹️ Controls / How to Play

Use the following keyboard shortcuts to control your character:

* **Move Left / Right:** Press `A` and `D` (or the Left and Right arrow keys)
* **Jump:** Press `W` or the `Spacebar`
* **Punch / Attack:** `Left-Click` with your mouse or press `J`
* **Dash:** Press the `Shift` key
